Copenhagen Jazz Festival

As one of the largest musical events in Europe and the world, the Copenhagen Jazz Festival has existed since 1964, and today it gathers over 250,000 people every year. Visitors choose from over a hundred venues which host a whopping 1100 concerts or more.

In my submission for a poster, I represent the spirit of the festival through iconography. The bicycle represents Copenhagen, famous for its cycling culture, the colors are typical of Denmark, and the trumpet shines as the focal element to represent the spirit and heritage of jazz.
